Once aidx is installed, a ✦ button sits in the corner of your dev app. It appears only when the
engine is live.

<ImageZoom src="/screenshots/widget-fab.png" alt="The aidx ✦ button in the corner of a running app" width={2560} height={1800} />

Click it to open the chat panel. From here you talk to the agent, watch it work, and answer its
prompts.

<ImageZoom src="/screenshots/chat-thread.png" alt="The aidx chat panel open over the app" width={760} height={1120} />
